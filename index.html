Ecovative

We create things using mushrooms.

Yes that’s right. Mushrooms.

How do we do it? That’s a great question.

  1. We take substrate and mix it with mycelium.
  2. We put this mixture in a tray, and wait as magic occurs.
  3. After about a week, the mixture has grown into one of our many products.

We can make mushroom chairs, mushroom airplanes, mushroom cats, and tons of other products.

We started in the basement of a university. Now we’re in a 10,000 sq ft. headquarters, making products for business partners all around the world.

If you’re a medium - large business looking to partner with us, please contact us and we can get the conversation started.
