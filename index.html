Ecovative

We create things using mushrooms.

Yes that’s right. Mushrooms.

We take substrate (corn husks, rice hulls, or whathaveyou). We mix it with mycelium. We add two parts magic & five parts science. And out comes Ecovative’s mushroom material.

We can make mushroom chairs, mushroom airplanes, mushroom cats, and tons of other products.

Ecovative was founded in 2007 by Eben Bayer and Gavin McIntyre.
